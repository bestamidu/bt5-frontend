Taylor Alison Swift (born December 13, 1989) is an American singer-songwriter. Known for her narrative songwriting and widely publicized life, she is one of the most popular musicians of her generation. Swift signed with Big Machine Records in 2005 and became a country music singer with the albums "Taylor Swift" (2006), "Fearless" (2008), and "Speak Now" (2010).
